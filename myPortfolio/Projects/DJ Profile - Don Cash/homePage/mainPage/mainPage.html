Don Cash has been in the industry since 2015, Influenced by his father (Radio and Club DJ); growing up surrounded with music on all kinds of Genre, 90’s, 2000’s to Present. His love with the Art and the Scene of DJing led him to improve every corner of his skills from technicality to crowd reading to connect and entertain all types of crowd.  

His skills has been proven and tested through the years, he has played a lot of private parties, clubs, bars, events. He has opened sets for numerous DJs from Manila, DJs Butta B, Marvelous, DJ Ace Ramos, Jeb Tizon, Hannah Ichiko. He is a former Resident DJ at Somewhere Else CDO, Kardinal Lifestyle District CDO, Blackout CDO, Superstar Club CDO, 4daboys Lounge and Pub CD, Watever KTV Bar CDO, a semi-finals champion CDO leg of San Mig Light DJ Spin Off 2019.
